# Contract Extraction

Pay special attention to:
- The contract number or reference may appear in the header or in the first paragraph.
- Effective and expiration dates may be expressed as "commencing on", "effective as of", "terminating on", "through", etc.
- Party names are usually introduced early: "This Agreement is entered into by and between [Party A] and [Party B]".
- Contract value may be expressed as a lump sum, recurring payment, or rate schedule.
- Governing law is typically near the end in a "Governing Law" or "Jurisdiction" clause.
- For terms_summary, extract the 2-3 most important obligations or conditions.
